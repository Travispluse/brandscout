## How to Use This List

These names are designed as inspiration — starting points for your own brainstorming. Some may be available; others won't be. The goal is to show you the patterns and techniques behind great brand names so you can create your own.

## Naming Patterns to Notice

Looking at these names, several patterns emerge:
- **Single real words** are consistently strong (Solace, Thrive, Breathe)
- **Metaphorical names** create rich associations (Scaffold, Prism, Trellis)
- **Sensory names** feel tangible and memorable (Bramble, Saffron, Ember)
- **Two-word combinations** create specificity (Stitch & Stone, Copper Kettle)
