# Brand Audit Checklist: Evaluate Your Brand's Health

A brand audit reveals the gap between how you want your brand to be perceived and how it's actually perceived. Whether you're considering a rebrand or optimizing your current brand, this checklist guides a thorough evaluation.

## Digital Presence Audit

### Domain and Website
- [ ] Primary domain is active and accessible
- [ ] SSL certificate is valid
- [ ] Website loads in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Mobile experience is optimized
- [ ] All pages have proper meta titles and descriptions
- [ ] Brand name is consistent across all pages
- [ ] Contact information is accurate and current

### Social Media Profiles
- [ ] Username is consistent across all platforms
- [ ] Profile images match current brand identity
- [ ] Bios are up-to-date and keyword-optimized
- [ ] All links point to correct destinations
- [ ] Inactive platforms are either updated or clearly marked

### Search Presence
- [ ] Brand ranks #1 for branded searches
- [ ] Google Knowledge Panel is accurate (if applicable)
- [ ] Google Business Profile is complete and current
- [ ] No negative content dominates search results

## Visual Identity Audit

- [ ] Logo is consistent across all touchpoints
- [ ] Color palette is used consistently
- [ ] Typography is consistent across materials
- [ ] Brand guidelines document exists and is current
- [ ] Visual identity works at all sizes (favicon to billboard)

## Brand Messaging Audit

- [ ] Value proposition is clear and compelling
- [ ] Brand voice is consistent across channels
- [ ] Key messages align with target audience needs
- [ ] Tagline/slogan is current and relevant
- [ ] Brand story is documented and authentic

## Competitive Positioning

- [ ] Competitive landscape is documented
- [ ] Brand differentiation is clear
- [ ] Pricing position aligns with brand perception
- [ ] Market positioning is defensible

## Customer Perception

- [ ] Customer reviews are monitored and responded to
- [ ] Net Promoter Score (NPS) is tracked
- [ ] Social mentions are monitored
- [ ] Customer feedback informs brand decisions

## Legal Protection

- [ ] Brand name is trademarked
- [ ] Domain portfolio includes defensive registrations
- [ ] Social media handles are secured
- [ ] Brand monitoring catches infringement early

## Action Items After Your Audit

Prioritize findings by impact and effort. Quick wins (updating outdated bios, fixing broken links) should be done immediately. Strategic changes (repositioning, visual refresh) require planning.
